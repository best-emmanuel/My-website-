Menu

  • Ofe Nsala (White Soup) — A light, spicy soup with catfish and yam.
  • Ofe Onugbu (Bitterleaf Soup) — Made with bitterleaf, cocoyam paste, and meat/fish.
  • Ofe Oha (Oha Soup) — A delicious soup with Oha leaves, cocoyam, and meats.
  • Ofe Akwu (Banga Soup) — Palm fruit extract soup, often with rice.
  • Okpa (Bambara Nut Pudding) — Protein-rich steamed snack from bambara flour.
